Comprehensive Vascular Services

Advanced surgical treatments for a full range of vascular conditions

Aneurysm Repair

Advanced surgical treatment for aortic and peripheral aneurysms using both open and endovascular techniques.

Conditions Treated: Aortic aneurysms, peripheral aneurysms, emergency repairs

Carotid Endarterectomy

Surgical removal of plaque from carotid arteries to prevent stroke and restore proper blood flow to the brain.

Benefits: Stroke prevention, improved blood flow, outpatient procedure options

Bypass Surgery

Creating alternative pathways for blood flow around blocked or narrowed arteries.

Techniques: Femoral-popliteal bypass, aorto-bifemoral bypass, arm access procedures

Angioplasty & Stenting

Minimally invasive procedures to open blocked arteries and maintain blood flow.

Advantages: Shorter recovery, outpatient procedure, immediate results

Varicose Vein Treatment

Modern treatments for varicose veins including endovenous laser therapy and sclerotherapy.

Options: Laser therapy, sclerotherapy, ambulatory phlebectomy

Peripheral Artery Disease

Comprehensive treatment for blocked arteries in arms and legs.

Treatments: Angioplasty, stenting, bypass surgery, medical management

Dialysis Access Surgery

Creating and maintaining vascular access for dialysis patients.

Services: AV fistula creation, graft placement, access maintenance

Deep Vein Thrombosis

Treatment and prevention of blood clots in deep veins.

Approaches: Thrombolysis, mechanical thrombectomy, anticoagulation management
